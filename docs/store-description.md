## Gaia Awakening

The world has gone quiet. The ore no longer waits in the cave walls where you left it; the old veins have sunk deep into the stone, and the machines that once tapped them stand silent and rusted. Gaia, the spirit of the world, is sleeping.

You and your friends arrive to wake her. You rebuild industry from a handful of coloured stone, relearn how to pull metal out of the earth, and climb gear by gear toward the Cathedral that will rouse Gaia from her slumber.

Gaia Awakening is a Create-focused kitchen-sink pack for NeoForge 1.21.1, made for small groups of friends and carried by a full, hand-written quest line. It is casual in difficulty and generous with guidance, but it asks one thing of you: forget everything you know about finding iron.

## Main Features

**A reworked ore economy.** Ore worldgen is gone. You will not strip-mine your way to diamonds here. You bootstrap with Create's coloured stones instead — Crimsite crushes into raw iron, Veridium into copper, Ochrum into gold, Asurine into zinc — then build a Drilling Machine and tap deep ore veins directly. Every drill head you craft, from the humble Basic bit to the apex Gaia-Infused head, opens up richer veins; bring a weak bit to a rich vein and you walk away empty-handed. Veins run dry, the world is full of more, and everything you pull still feeds the full Create and Mekanism processing chain for up to sixteenfold output.

**A quest line that actually teaches.** Over a hundred quests walk you from your first crafting table to the endgame, one mechanic at a time. They are written to inform first and entertain second, so newcomers never feel lost and veterans can skim ahead.

**Loot you earn, not farm.** The best drill heads and endgame recipes are gated behind Resource and Mythic Catalysts, currencies that drop from Cataclysm's apex bosses. There are no free shortcuts to the top of the tech tree.

**The Gaia Cathedral.** The final tier is a build, not a grind. Raise the Cathedral megastructure, wire in a Mekanism fusion reactor, defeat the boss trio, and wake Gaia. That is the win condition.

## Overview

Everything is built around **Create** and its add-ons — Steam 'n' Rails, Create New Age, Enchantment Industry, Garnished, Diesel Generators and Ore Excavation — with heavy industry handled by **Mekanism**, digital storage by **Applied Energistics 2**, and pressure tech by **PneumaticCraft**.

The magic side runs through **Ars Nouveau** and **Apotheosis**, with **Occultism** as a darker optional path, while **L_Ender's Cataclysm** and **Mowzie's Mobs** bring the boss fights. The world itself is rebuilt by **Terralith** and **Tectonic** — over a hundred biomes, peaks past Y=500 and caves running deep below zero — with **Serene Seasons** and **Farmer's Delight** filling in daily life.

Quality of life comes from **Sophisticated Storage**, **Waystones**, **FTB Chunks**, **Ultimine** and **JourneyMap**, and it all runs on a full optimization stack (**Embeddium**, **Iris Shaders**, **Distant Horizons**, **ModernFix**) so a mid-range PC stays smooth and a strong one looks gorgeous.

## Known Issues (Beta)

This is the first public build, so expect a few rough edges while balance and quests settle in.

- On the vein-finder radar, modded ores that aren't vanilla (osmium, tin, lead, silver and a few others) all share one generic marker colour. That's a cosmetic limit of the finder mod, not a problem with your world.
- Vein sizes, drop rates and quest rewards are still being tuned and may change between beta builds.

Found a bug or a balance problem? Please open an issue on GitHub: <https://github.com/VugasDev/gaia-awakening/issues>

### On the use of AI

In the spirit of fairness: AI was part of the toolset used to build this pack. It helped write and debug the custom KubeJS systems behind the ore progression, draft the quest line, and generate the logo and key art. Every part of it was directed, reviewed and tested by a human before it shipped.
