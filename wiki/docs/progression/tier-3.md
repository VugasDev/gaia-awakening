# Tier 3: Energy and Automation

Energy and Automation is the transition from mechanical systems to digital ones. You bridge Create rotation into Forge Energy, build your first ME network, raise air-pressure infrastructure with PneumaticCraft, grow resources with Mystical Agriculture, and take your first steps into Ars Nouveau magic.

## Theme

Power everything, store everything digitally, and grow materials rather than mine them. This is the tier where the pack opens up into parallel specialisations: technology (AE2 + Mekanism), magic (Ars Nouveau / Occultism) and farming (Mystical Agriculture).

## Chapters

### Power Up
Bridges Create and Mekanism. The Create: Crafts and Additions **Alternator** converts shaft rotation into Forge Energy; the **Electric Motor** does the reverse. This is gated on having both a Precision Mechanism (T2 Create) and a Basic Energy Cube (T2 Mekanism).

Additional power options unlocked here:

- **Powah** — scalable FE generators (Furnators, Thermo Generators, Solar Panels) upgradeable through six tiers
- **XNet** — a single-cable network that routes items, fluids and energy simultaneously; one controller can manage your entire base
- Optional: Create: New Age generator coils, Mekanism Solar and Wind Generators

### Going Digital
Applied Energistics 2 from first crystal to full autocrafting. Farm Certus Quartz from budding blocks (accelerate with Growth Accelerators), charge it, fuse it into Fluix Crystal, stamp processors in the Inscriber, build an ME Controller and connect a Terminal plus Drive.

Autocrafting (Pattern Providers + Molecular Assemblers) is the biggest quality-of-life jump in the pack: encode recipes, request items, the network delivers them on demand.

Note: the ME Controller requires a **Create Precision Mechanism** and **Create Propeller** in its crafting recipe (custom balance gate) — this ensures players have progressed through T2 Create before reaching digital storage.

### Under Pressure
PneumaticCraft infrastructure. Make Compressed Iron (drop iron beside TNT — literally), build an Air Compressor and pressure tube network, then construct a **Pressure Chamber** (hollow cube with valve and interface). The Pressure Chamber crafts more Compressed Iron safely and is the foundation for the custom pressure-enchanting system that unlocks in Tier 4.

Optional quests: Vortex Tube (temperature control), Amadron Tablet (drone delivery marketplace).

### Mystical Farming
Mystical Agriculture seed infrastructure. Collect Prosperity Shards, grow Inferium Essence, build the **Infusion Altar** with eight pedestals, and craft your first metal seeds (iron, coal — Tier 2 seeds).

An important note from the Tier 2 seed quest: **Tier 3 seeds** (diamond, emerald, gold, glowstone) require a Resource Catalyst. Obtaining that item is the focus of the Tier 4 "The Catalyst" chapter.

Optional quest: Seed Reprocessor (converts unwanted seeds back to essence).

### School of Magic
Ars Nouveau introduction. Read the Worn Notebook, craft a Novice Spell Book and Scribes Table (custom spells from glyphs), establish a Source supply via an Agronomic Sourcelink, build an Imbuement Chamber and Enchanting Apparatus.

The chapter closes with the **Apprentice Spell Book** — the mid-tier spellbook. The Archmage tier comes in T4.

Optional quests: Starbuncle item transport, Iron's Spells 'n' Spellbooks scrolls (second magic system found in dungeons), Occultism summoning (Dictionary of Spirits — an alternative dimensional storage system).

## Key Mods

- **Create: Crafts and Additions** — Alternator (rotation to FE), Electric Motor (FE to rotation)
- **Powah** — scalable FE generators and batteries
- **XNet** — unified item/fluid/energy cable network
- **Applied Energistics 2** — ME network, digital storage, autocrafting
- **PneumaticCraft** — Compressed Iron, Air Compressor, Pressure Chamber
- **Mystical Agriculture** — Prosperity Shards, Inferium seeds, Infusion Altar, Tier 1–2 seeds
- **Ars Nouveau** — Worn Notebook, spell system, Apprentice Spell Book
- **Iron's Spells 'n' Spellbooks** — scroll-based magic (optional)
- **Occultism** — spirit summoning and dimensional storage (optional)

## Milestone: Unlocking Tier 4

Complete AE2 autocrafting ("Autocrafting" quest) and build the XNet controller. The first T4 chapter, Heavy Industry, requires the AE2 Pattern Provider network and XNet to be in place.
