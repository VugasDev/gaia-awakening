# Tier 4: Advanced Systems

Advanced Systems is the deepest tier in the pack. It covers heavy Mekanism processing, MEGA-scale AE2 storage, the Resource Catalyst chain for Tier 3 Mystical Agriculture seeds, magical automation with Ars Nouveau Drygmys, and the custom pressure-enchanting system.

## Theme

Everything runs at scale. Ore processing goes to x3 and potentially x4, storage moves from kilobytes to megabytes, seeds grow diamonds and emeralds, Drygmys farm bosses passively, and enchantments exceed vanilla limits via the PneumaticCraft Pressure Chamber.

## Chapters

### Heavy Industry
Mekanism's mid-game chapter. The **Purification Chamber** uses oxygen (from an Electrolytic Separator splitting water) to process raw ore into three clumps instead of two — the x3 ore chain. The full chain: Purification Chamber → Crusher → Enrichment Chamber → Energized Smelter.

Higher milestones:

- **Advanced Alloys** — Reinforced Alloy (diamond infusion) and Atomic Alloy (Refined Obsidian infusion); these gate the Digital Miner, Mekanism teleporters and the Fusion Reactor
- **Refined Obsidian** — required for Atomic Alloy and the custom Refined Obsidian Drill Head

Optional quests: Mekanism Jetpack (hydrogen-powered hover flight), Atomic Disassembler (one tool replacing pickaxe, shovel, axe, sword and hoe), and the Chemistry Lab (x4 ore chain via Chemical Injection Chamber).

### Mass Storage
AE2 scaled up with MEGA Cells. Cells go from 1M to 64M items (and eventually 256M in T5). MEGA Cells are built from Sky Steel found in meteorites. The Quantum Network Bridge (optional) links ME networks across any distance, including across dimensions.

### The Catalyst
The Resource Catalyst chapter. This is where Tier 3 Mystical Agriculture seeds become available.

The **Resource Catalyst** (`apex:resource_catalyst`) can be obtained three ways:

1. **Passive** — 0.5% drop per ore from Create Ore Excavation vein drills
2. **Crafting** — Mechanical Crafter recipe using three essence types and an Apotheosis gem
3. **Active** — boss drops (Mowzie's bosses, Netherite Monstrosity), dungeon loot (8–10%), Catalyst Altar (1000 mB Hyper Experience via Create Spout), Wandering Trader (15%)

With a Resource Catalyst you can craft **Tier 3 seeds**: diamond, emerald, gold, glowstone.

The chapter also introduces **Create Ore Excavation** drill infrastructure and a series of custom Drill Heads:

- Osmium Drill Head — faster vein drilling
- Refined Obsidian Drill Head — Tier 4 workhorse (gated on Refined Obsidian from Heavy Industry)
- Crystal Drill Head — specialised for certus, amethyst, fluorite veins (optional)
- Nether Drill Head — specialised for quartz, gold, ancient debris (optional)
- Catalyst Altar — converts Hyper Experience into catalysts (automatable via Create Spout)

### Magical Automation
Ars Nouveau's late-game chapter, gated on reaching Apprentice Spell Book in T3.

The **Drygmy** is the centrepiece: it channels nearby mobs to produce their drops without killing them. Build a Drygmy enclosure with a steady Source supply, add mob entities, and it runs passively. Capturing a Cataclysm boss in the enclosure later generates **Mythic Catalyst** materials — the gate to Tier 4 seeds.

This chapter also unlocks the **Archmage Spell Book** (maximum mana and glyph slots) and optional magical automation tools: Whirlisprig crop harvest, Wixie potion brewing.

### Beyond Vanilla Enchanting
The custom enchanting system. This chapter requires the Pressure Chamber from T3 PneumaticCraft.

- **Apotheosis Gems** — stat-bearing gems drop from mobs; socket them into gear at a Smithing Table using a Sigil of Socketing. Epic and Mythic rarity gems give significant bonuses
- **Liquid XP** — Create: Enchantment Industry turns experience into a fluid via the Experience Rotor. Condense it into Hyper Experience for the Catalyst Altar and high-tier enchanting
- **Pressure Enchanting** — the PneumaticCraft Pressure Chamber pushes enchantments past vanilla caps. Three pressure tiers (3.5, 5.5 and 7.5 bar) unlock enchantment levels up to "GODLIKE." Higher pressures require materials from T4 and T5

## Key Mods

- **Mekanism** — Purification Chamber (x3 ores), Advanced Alloys, Refined Obsidian, Jetpack, Atomic Disassembler
- **AE2 + MEGA Cells** — storage cells up to 64M items, Quantum Network Bridge
- **Create Ore Excavation** — infinite vein drilling; custom Drill Heads
- **Ars Nouveau** — Drygmy farm, Archmage Spell Book
- **Create: Enchantment Industry** — Liquid XP, Hyper Experience
- **PneumaticCraft** — pressure-based enchanting beyond vanilla limits
- **Apotheosis** — gem socketing, high-rarity gem bonuses

## Milestone: Unlocking Tier 5

Complete **Advanced Alloys** (Atomic Alloy) in Heavy Industry, reach **64M MEGA Cell** in Mass Storage, and build a **working Drygmy farm**. These three gates unlock the first Tier 5 chapter, Apex Predators.
