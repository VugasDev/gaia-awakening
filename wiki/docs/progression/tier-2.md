# Tier 2: Brass and Machines

Brass and Machines is where Create becomes your primary tool. You smelt brass, build smart logistics, start Mekanism's material chain, and organise the storage systems that will carry you through the rest of the pack.

## Theme

Capture a blaze, unlock brass, build automated factories and start processing ores with Mekanism. The tier also introduces dedicated storage with Functional Storage drawers and Sophisticated Storage chests.

## Chapters

### The Brass Age
The core Create chapter for T2. Right-click a Blaze with an empty Blaze Burner to capture it, then use it under a heated Mixer to smelt brass from copper and zinc. Brass Casings unlock the smart side of Create — precise machines, smart funnels and Mechanical Crafters.

Key milestones in this chapter:

- **Electron Tubes** — the circuit component for all advanced Create machines
- **Mechanical Crafter** — a 3x3 automated crafting grid; you will need a 9-block setup for some custom pack recipes (including the Resource Catalyst later)
- **Precision Mechanism** — assembled in stages on a Depot with a Deployer; this component gates almost all advanced tech in the pack, including the Mekanism Enrichment Chamber
- **Mechanical Arm** — the final Create logistics piece; automates item movement between any input and output

Optional quests cover brass-tier sorting (Brass Funnels, Brass Tunnels), the Create train system (Steam 'n' Rails), and the Deployer tool.

### Green Machines
Mekanism's entry chapter. Mine osmium, build the Metallurgic Infuser to smelt steel, and unlock the **Enrichment Chamber**. The Enrichment Chamber requires both Mekanism Steel and a Create Precision Mechanism (custom recipe gate) — a deliberate balance decision to ensure players have worked through the Create chain first.

The chapter walks through the x2 ore processing chain: Enrichment Chamber produces dust, which smelts into double ingots. A Basic Energy Cube stores Forge Energy from your early generators. Optional quests introduce the Heat Generator (passive lava power), the Mekanism Fluid Tank and the Configurator.

### Stay Organized
Storage infrastructure. Functional Storage drawers hold large quantities of a single item type; the Storage Controller links an entire drawer wall to one access point. Sophisticated Storage chests and barrels accept the same upgrade modules as Sophisticated Backpacks (auto-smelt, stack upgrades, magnet).

Optional quests cover the Simple Magnet and Carry On (picks up entire blocks and mobs).

### Chef's Table
A short Farmer's Delight chapter gated on completing the "Farm to Table" optional quest in T1. Covers the iron knife, Cutting Board (doubles food output from harvests), and Rich Soil (accelerates crop growth — especially useful for Mystical Agriculture later).

## Key Mods

- **Create** (Brass tier) — Blaze Burner, Mechanical Crafter, Precision Mechanism, Mechanical Arm
- **Steam 'n' Rails** — train network (optional)
- **Mekanism** — Metallurgic Infuser, steel, Enrichment Chamber (x2 ore processing)
- **Functional Storage** — drawer walls and Storage Controller
- **Sophisticated Storage** — upgradeable chests and barrels
- **Farmer's Delight** — knife, Cutting Board, Rich Soil

## Milestone: Unlocking Tier 3

Build a **Precision Mechanism** and a **Basic Energy Cube**. These two items gate the first T3 chapter (Power Up), because T3 requires you to bridge Create rotation into Forge Energy via Create Crafts and Additions — and that requires both the Create chain and Mekanism fundamentals to be in place.
