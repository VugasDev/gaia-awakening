# Tier 1: The Beginning

The opening tier eases you into Gaia Awakening. Survival is the immediate goal, but T1 also introduces the Create mod at its most fundamental level and pushes you to explore a world that is significantly taller, wilder and more dangerous than vanilla Minecraft.

## Theme

Handcraft everything, learn the basics of Create rotation mechanics, gather Nether resources, and get comfortable with the expanded world generation (Tectonic + Terralith — over 100 biomes, peaks up to Y=512, underground rivers down to Y=-128).

## Chapters

### First Steps
The survival chapter. Build a crafting table, smelt your first iron, craft iron armour and push into the Nether by the end. Key quests cover the furnace, diamonds, an enchanting table, Waystones for fast travel, and a Nether portal to collect blaze rods and quartz — both required for the Brass Age.

Optional side quests introduce Farmer's Delight cooking, Serene Seasons crop planning, FTB Chunks land claiming, and FTB Ultimine for vein mining.

### The Andesite Age
Create at the Andesite tier. Start with Andesite Alloy, learn shafts and cogwheels, build Water Wheels for your first power source, and work through the Press, Mixer, Crushing Wheels and belt/funnel logistics. The chapter closes with Andesite Casing — the graduation milestone for T1 Create.

Optional quests cover the Windmill (more stress capacity than water wheels), Engineer's Goggles and Wrench, and a basic item sorting setup with filters.

The Crushing Wheels quest introduces ore doubling: crush raw ore, wash it with an Encased Fan, and you get extra nuggets for free. This is the APEX Ore Chain T2 (x2 multiplier).

### Into the Wild
Exploration. Craft the Nature's Compass to locate biomes and the Explorer's Compass to find structures. The chapter ends with your first real boss fight: the **Ferrous Wroughtnaut** (Mowzie's Mobs), which lurks in underground chambers.

Optional quests reward clearing a large When Dungeons Arise structure, hunting the Frostmaw in frozen biomes, finding Artifacts in dungeon loot (they slot into Curios accessory slots), and opening Lootr chests for per-player loot.

## Key Mods

- **Create** (Andesite tier) — rotation power, ore processing, logistics
- **Apotheosis** — improved enchanting table setup (T1 enchants up to level 10)
- **Waystones** — fast travel network
- **FTB Ultimine / FTB Chunks** — QoL mining and land claiming
- **Farmer's Delight** — better food and saturation (Nourishment buff)
- **Mowzie's Mobs** — first boss content
- **Artifacts** — Curios-slot trinkets found in dungeons
- **Sophisticated Backpacks** — upgradeable inventory (magnet upgrade reward for completing T1)

## Milestone: Unlocking Tier 2

Collect **blaze rods** and **quartz** from the Nether. Completing the "Blaze It" quest in First Steps and the "Andesite Casing" quest in The Andesite Age signals you are ready to enter the Brass Age.
