# Exploration & Adventure Mods

Exploration in Gaia Awakening is built around a high world height (800 blocks), a large pre-generated map radius, and a density of dangerous structures that reward you with gear and loot unavailable from crafting alone. This category covers world generation, dungeon structures, creatures, bosses, and navigation.

---

## World Generation

### Terralith

**Terralith** replaces vanilla biome generation with around 100 new biomes — volcanic peaks, ancient sands, yellowstone-style geysers, and more. It runs on top of vanilla terrain without requiring new dimensions. Most overworld exploration happens within Terralith biomes.

### Tectonic

**Tectonic** amplifies terrain scale: mountains reach far higher, valleys are deeper, and continental shelves are wider. It is essential for supporting the pack's 800-block world height. Pairs with Terralith for dramatic, varied landscapes.

### Lithostitched

**Lithostitched** is a world-gen library that helps mods like Terralith and Tectonic stitch together cleanly without chunk-border seams.

## Dungeon & Structure Mods

### When Dungeons Arise

**When Dungeons Arise** generates large, multi-floor dungeons and towers throughout the overworld — flying fortresses, desert temples, and underground labyrinths. These structures contain better loot than vanilla but are guarded by stronger mobs. They are a key source of early Apotheosis gems and Artifacts.

### YUNG's Structure Improvements

The YUNG's suite replaces vanilla structures with significantly more detailed, better-loot versions:

| Mod | What it improves |
|---|---|
| **YUNG's Better Dungeons** | Vanilla dungeons — multi-room layouts with traps and boss chests. |
| **YUNG's Better Mineshafts** | Mineshafts — branching tunnels with supply caches. |
| **YUNG's Better Strongholds** | End Strongholds — multi-wing layouts with new rooms. |
| **YUNG's Better Witch Huts** | Witch huts — expanded layout with brewing setups. |

## Creatures & Bosses

### Alex's Mobs

**Alex's Mobs** adds over 80 new creatures to every biome — creatures like Grizzly Bears, Crocodiles, Lobsters, and Roadrunners. Each mob has custom AI and drops useful items. The pack uses the unofficial NeoForge 1.21.1 port.

### Alex's Caves

**Alex's Caves** adds five unique underground biomes (Toxic Caves, Magnetic Caves, Primordial Caves, Crystal Caves, Abyssal Chasm) each with unique mobs, ores, and environmental hazards. Exploring all five is part of the quest line's underground chapter.

### Mowzie's Mobs

**Mowzie's Mobs** adds a small set of hand-crafted, heavily animated boss-tier creatures: the Frostmaw ice giant, the Grottol gem-slime, the Barako sun chief, and others. Each has unique attack patterns and drops unique gear. The GeckoLib animation library (included) powers their custom animations.

### L_Ender's Cataclysm

**L_Ender's Cataclysm** provides epic endgame boss encounters — the Ignited Revenant (Ignis) and the Ancient Remnant (Harbinger) among others. These bosses are the primary source of the pack's highest-tier loot drops. Boss drop tables are customised via KubeJS LootJS scripts to fit the Catalyst System.

### Artifacts

**Artifacts** scatters rare accessory items throughout dungeon chests — Bunny Feet, Cross Necklace, Kitty Slippers, and similar unique items. These are worn in Curios slots and provide passive bonuses that do not require any crafting.

## Navigation

### Waystones

**Waystones** places stone waystone blocks in villages and other structures. Right-clicking one registers it; registered waystones can then be teleported to from anywhere. Essential for a large pre-generated map — without waystones, returning to a distant base or dungeon is impractical.

### Explorer's Compass

**Explorer's Compass** lets you search for any structure type in the world and pinpoint its nearest location. Use it to hunt down specific dungeons, mineshafts, or Cataclysm boss arenas without wandering blindly.

### Nature's Compass

**Nature's Compass** does the same for biomes — point it at a biome type to find the nearest one. Useful for locating Terralith-exclusive biomes required by Serene Seasons quests.

### JourneyMap

**JourneyMap** provides an in-game minimap and full-screen world map. It runs automatically in the background, recording explored terrain as you move. Waypoints can be set manually or imported from Waystones.

## Additional Adventure Content

### Lootr

**Lootr** makes all loot chests in structures personal — each player gets their own randomised loot instead of competing over a single chest. Prevents the first player to explore a dungeon from emptying it for the group.

### Supplementaries

**Supplementaries** adds decorative and functional vanilla-style blocks (rope, notice boards, jars, weather vanes) found as loot and used in quest builds.

### Simple Magnets

**Simple Magnets** adds an item magnet accessory (worn in a Curios slot) that pulls nearby dropped items to you automatically. A quality-of-life item for any exploration build.
