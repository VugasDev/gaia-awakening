# Magic Mods

Magic in Gaia Awakening runs parallel to the tech path from Tier 2 onward. The two main pillars are **Ars Nouveau** (active spell-casting) and **Apotheosis** (enchantment overhaul). Occultism and Iron's Spells provide additional depth for players who want to invest further.

---

## Ars Nouveau

**Ars Nouveau** is the primary magic mod. You craft a Spellbook, then combine **Glyphs** (spell components) into custom spells — projectile fire, self-heal, blink teleport, or anything the glyph library allows. Glyphs are researched through an Arcane Compendium and require Source (the mod's mana equivalent), which is generated by Imbuement Chambers and Source Jars placed near flowers or trees.

Ars Nouveau opens up at Tier 2 once you have access to the materials needed to craft the Arcane Core.

### Ars Nouveau addons

| Mod | Role |
|---|---|
| **Ars Additions** | Adds quality-of-life glyphs, additional Source generators, and expanded spell options. |
| **Ars Creo** | Bridges Ars Nouveau and Create — lets you use Create rotational force to generate Source and adds Create-themed spell glyphs. The explicit link between the two main progression systems. |

---

## Apotheosis

**Apotheosis** overhauls the enchanting system. It removes the vanilla level cap on enchantments, adds Gem Socketing (insert gems into gear for passive bonuses), introduces new enchantment types, and adds dungeon bosses that drop high-tier gear and gems.

In this pack, Apotheosis is a **mandatory progression gate**: you cannot reach the higher Mekanism tool tiers or PneumaticCraft enchantment levels without first progressing through Apotheosis enchanting.

!!! info "Enchantment system"
    Apotheosis handles Tier 1-2 enchanting (levels 1-10). PneumaticCraft and Create: Enchantment Industry extend this further. See the [Enchantment System](../systems/enchantments.md) for the complete workflow.

### Apotheosis addons

| Mod | Role |
|---|---|
| **Apotheosis: Compatibility (Compat)** | Extends Apotheosis gem socketing and enchantment support to items from other mods (Create tools, Mekanism armour, etc.). |
| **Apothic Enchanting** | Adds the Enchanting Library, Altar of Experience, and Scribe's Quill — tools for storing, transferring, and upgrading enchantments. |
| **Apothic Attributes** | Adds attribute modifiers (max health, attack speed, luck, etc.) that can be applied through gems and enchantments. |
| **Apothic Spawners** | Lets you capture and modify mob spawners, useful for controlled XP farming once you reach Tier 3. |
| **Enigmatic Legacy+** | Adds powerful artefact items found in the world (rings, amulets, charms with unique effects). An optional progression layer for players who enjoy item collection. |

## Occultism

**Occultism** is a demon-summoning magic mod. You bind demons into physical forms (Djinni, Afrit, Marid) and command them to perform tasks — item transport, mining, crafting, or guarding. It also adds a **Dimensional Storage** system (a personal pocket dimension for bulk storage).

Occultism was chosen as the secondary magic path after Botania was dropped (no NeoForge 1.21.1 port). It is optional but provides a unique automation approach that differs from both Create and Mekanism.

---

## Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks

**Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks** adds combat-focused spell classes (Mage, Warlock, Necromancer) with spell scrolls found in dungeons and towers. It complements Ars Nouveau's crafted spells with pre-found, class-gated combat abilities. Good for players who prefer gear-based magic over the Ars glyph crafting system.
