# Mods by Category

Gaia Awakening ships around 100 mods that cover everything from factory automation to spell-crafting to boss fights. This section groups them into six thematic categories so you can find what you are looking for quickly and understand how each group fits into the broader progression.

## How the groups are organised

| Category | What you'll find |
|---|---|
| [Tech](tech.md) | Create, Mekanism, Applied Energistics 2, PneumaticCraft, XNet, Powah! |
| [Magic](magic.md) | Ars Nouveau, Apotheosis, Occultism, Iron's Spells |
| [Exploration & Adventure](exploration.md) | Dungeons, structures, mobs, bosses, fast-travel |
| [Farming & Food](farming-food.md) | Mystical Agriculture, Farmer's Delight, Serene Seasons |
| [Storage & QoL](storage-qol.md) | Sophisticated Backpacks/Storage, Carry On, REI, helper mods |
| [Performance & Libraries](performance-libraries.md) | Rendering optimisers, world-gen patches, shared library mods |

## Progression relationship

Most mods in this pack are gated through the quest book. A rough dependency order is:

1. **Tech (Create)** — your first factory, Tier 1–2
2. **Magic (Ars Nouveau / Apotheosis)** — unlocks alongside Create at Tier 2–3
3. **Tech (Mekanism → AE2)** — Tier 3–5, requires Create components as gate items
4. **Farming / Exploration** — available at any point but reward scales with gear
5. **Storage** — grows with your base across all tiers

Cross-references appear where mods overlap categories (for example, Mystical Agriculture spans both the Farming and the resource-generation side of Tech).
