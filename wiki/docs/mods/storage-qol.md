# Storage & Quality of Life Mods

This category covers inventory management, item lookup, and the small mods that make day-to-day play smoother. None of these are progression gates — they are tools that reduce friction as your base and item diversity grow.

---

## Sophisticated Backpacks / Storage / Core

The Sophisticated suite provides configurable inventory expansion at every stage of the game.

| Mod | What it provides |
|---|---|
| **Sophisticated Core** | Shared library required by the other two. Provides the upgrade system (filter, void, auto-pickup, sorting, tank, compacting upgrades). |
| **Sophisticated Backpacks** | Wearable backpacks (worn in the Back Curios slot) with up to 4 upgrade slots. Invaluable for dungeon runs and mining trips — filter upgrades auto-sort loot by type. |
| **Sophisticated Storage** | Chests, barrels, and shulker boxes with the same upgrade system as backpacks. Use them as pre-AE2 bulk storage, or alongside AE2 for overflow. |

**Recommended progression:** Start with a Basic Backpack at Tier 1. Add a Pickup Upgrade immediately for auto-looting. Upgrade to Iron/Gold Backpack at Tier 2. Sophisticated Storage chests replace plain chests once you have enough iron and copper to craft upgrades.

## Carry On

**Carry On** lets you pick up and carry chests (with their contents intact), machines, and passive mobs by holding Shift and right-clicking them. Useful for reorganising your base without emptying every chest or breaking and replacing machines.

!!! warning
    You cannot carry a chest while a mob or player is inside it, and some machines may lose internal tank/energy contents depending on mod configuration.

## Roughly Enough Items (REI)

**REI** is the item and recipe browser. Press `R` on any item to see all recipes that produce it; press `U` to see all uses of an item. REI is configured in this pack to hide intermediate KubeJS items that are not meant to be crafted directly (via KubeJS REI Integration).

### Searchables

**Searchables** is a library that powers REI's advanced search syntax — type `@create` to filter by mod, `#stone` to filter by tag, `$tool` to filter by item type. It also improves search performance in large item lists.

## JourneyMap

**JourneyMap** runs as an automatic minimap (top-right corner) and full-screen world map (`J`). It records terrain as you explore and supports manual waypoints. Covered in more depth on the [Exploration & Adventure](exploration.md) page.

---

## Jade

**Jade** shows a heads-up display in the top-centre of the screen when you look at a block or entity. It displays: block/entity name, mod origin, energy or fluid storage levels (for machines), health bars (for mobs), and crop growth stage. Extremely useful for debugging machine connections and checking storage levels without opening a GUI.

---

## Inventory Profiles Next (IPN)

**IPN** adds an inventory sort button (small icon in your inventory screen). One click sorts your inventory or a chest by item type and count. Also supports locked slots (prevent a slot from being overwritten during sort) and a refill-from-chest feature. Requires **libIPN** (included).

---

## Curios API

**Curios** adds extra equipment slots for accessories — back, belt, charm, necklace, ring, head. Many mods in this pack use Curios slots: Sophisticated Backpacks (back), Simple Magnets (belt), Artifacts and Relics items (charm/ring/necklace). Open the Curios screen with `G` or the dedicated button in your inventory.

## Other QoL Mods

| Mod | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Clumps** | Merges nearby XP orbs into a single large orb. Reduces entity count and lag in XP-heavy areas (Apotheosis spawners, Create Enchantment Industry). |
| **Controlling** | Adds a search bar to the Controls settings screen so you can find and fix keybind conflicts quickly. |
| **AppleSkin** | Shows exact hunger, saturation, and health-restore values on the HUD while holding food. Useful for optimising Farmer's Delight meals. |
| **Mouse Tweaks** | Improves inventory mouse interactions: drag items across slots to distribute, right-click-drag to split stacks, scroll wheel to move items. |
| **FTB Ultimine** | Hold a configurable key while mining to vein-mine an entire connected ore vein. Configurable per tool type and ore tag. |
| **FTB Chunks** | Claim chunks to prevent other players from building or breaking in your base area. Also enables chunk loading (active even when you are offline) for unattended machines. |
| **Just Zoom** | Adds an adjustable zoom key (default `Z`). Scroll the mouse wheel while zoomed to increase/decrease magnification. |
| **Toast Control** | Suppresses or re-routes the advancement toast popups that appear in the top-right corner, so they do not overlap with JourneyMap or other HUD elements. |
| **Durability Viewer** | Adds a small HUD showing remaining durability on your held tool and armour pieces without opening inventory. |
| **Configured** | Adds a Config menu entry for every loaded mod that supports standard config formats, accessible from the Mods screen in-game. |
| **Sound Physics Remastered** | Adds reverb and occlusion to sounds — caves echo, sounds through walls are muffled. Purely atmospheric. |
| **Chipped** | Adds many variants of vanilla blocks (stone, wood, glass) for building. Craft at a workbench-tier station using vanilla blocks. |
| **Macaw's** suite | Seven mods adding varied windows, doors, roofs, bridges, fences, trapdoors, and lights. Purely decorative building options. |
| **Supplementaries** | Vanilla-style functional decorations — rope, signs, jars, flags, etc. |
| **Lootr** | Per-player loot chests in structures so every player gets their own roll. |
