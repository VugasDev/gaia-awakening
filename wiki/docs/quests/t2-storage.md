# Stay Organized

**Tier 2 — Chapter 3 of 4**

Chest walls collapse at scale. This chapter introduces Functional Storage drawers, Sophisticated Storage's upgradeable chests, and quality-of-life utilities that keep your inventory under control as item counts explode in the later tiers.

Requires: `t1_survival` quest 7 (Diamonds) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Drawers** | Craft 4 two-slot oak drawers to hold large quantities of single item types (cobble, ingots, crops). |
| 2 | **Storage Controller** | Connect your drawer wall to a Storage Controller — interact with all linked drawers through one block. |
| 3 | **Sophisticated Storage** | Craft and upgrade a Sophisticated chest or barrel to access stack upgrades, auto-smelting, and compacting. |

---

## Optional Side Quests (Diamond)

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Magnet Time** (n=4) | 8 Gold Ingots, 100 XP | Craft a Simple Magnet and toggle it to pull dropped items to you without moving. |
| **Just Carry It** (n=5) | 1 Ender Chest, 100 XP | Use Carry On to sneak + right-click and relocate a chest, machine or even a mob with your bare hands. |

---

## Tips

- **Double-click** a Functional Storage drawer to deposit all matching items from your inventory at once.
- The **Storage Controller** accepts thrown items (drop them toward it) and sorts them automatically — no sorting pipes needed.
- Sophisticated Storage takes the **same upgrades as Sophisticated Backpacks**: stack compression, feeding, auto-smelting, and more. Upgrade tiers dramatically increase capacity — a Netherite-tier chest holds an enormous amount.
- The **Ender Chest** reward from "Just Carry It" pairs with Sophisticated Storage for cross-base item access.
- This chapter's investment pays off when AE2 arrives in Tier 3: well-organized pre-digital storage makes the ME migration much cleaner.
