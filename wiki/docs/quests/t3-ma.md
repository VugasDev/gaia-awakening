# Mystical Farming

**Tier 3 — Chapter 4 of 5**

Mystical Agriculture makes resources farmable: ore seeds grow ingot essences, which convert into the real item. This chapter establishes your growing infrastructure — Prosperity Shards, Inferium, an Infusion Altar, and your first metal seeds. Tier 3 and Tier 4 seeds require the Resource Catalyst (a Tier 4 system), but the groundwork starts here.

Requires: `t1_survival` quest 7 (Diamonds) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Prosperity** | Mine 8 Prosperity Shards from underground prosperity ore — the foundation material for all seeds. |
| 2 | **Inferium** | Collect 32 Inferium Essence and plant Inferium Seeds; these are the base of the five-tier essence chain. |
| 3 | **Infusion Altar** | Build an Infusion Altar with 8 Pedestals to craft higher-tier seeds and essences. |
| 4 | **Tier 2 Seeds** | Craft your first Tier 2 metal seed (iron or coal recommended as starting points). |

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |
|-------|---------|-----------|

## Tips

- **Rich Soil** from `t2_kitchen` quest 2 accelerates crop growth noticeably. Cover your entire Mystical Agriculture farm in Rich Soil before planting.
- Inferium seeds need only basic farmland to start. Once established, the essence supply for upgrading tiers is largely self-sustaining.
- **Tier 3 seeds** (diamond, emerald, gold, glowstone) require the **Resource Catalyst** — a Tier 4 custom item. The game will tell you this in the quest description for quest 4. Plan ahead.
- The essence tiers are: Inferium → Prudentium → Tertium → Imperium → Supremium. Higher essences take more of the tier below to craft.
- The **Seed Reprocessor** is worth building before you reach Tier 4, when seed turnover accelerates significantly.
