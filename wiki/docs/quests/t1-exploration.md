# Into the Wild

**Tier 1 — Chapter 3 of 3**

Gaia Awakening's world is enormous: over 100 biomes from Tectonic and Terralith, mountains reaching Y=512, underground rivers, and colossal dungeons from When Dungeons Arise. This chapter equips you to navigate it all — and survive the bosses lurking inside.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Nature's Compass** | Craft a Nature's Compass to locate any of the 100+ biomes in the world. |
| 2 | **Explorer's Compass** | Craft an Explorer's Compass to find structures: dungeons, bastions, strongholds, and modded landmarks. |
| 6 | **Ferrous Wroughtnaut** | Defeat Mowzie's Ferrous Wroughtnaut in its underground chamber — your first real boss encounter. |

## Tips

- The **Explorer's Compass** (quest 2) can locate AE2 meteorites — useful much later when you need Inscriber Presses. Bookmark this tool.
- **Ferrous Wroughtnaut** (quest 6): attack its back after it overswings. Patience is more effective than aggression.
- **When Dungeons Arise** structures spawn elite mobs and complex traps. Bring fire resistance potions, food, and a full iron kit before your first raid.
- **Lootr** (side quest 3) means loot chests are instanced per player — no more racing teammates to the chest. Every naturally generated chest qualifies.
- The **Waystone Network** side quest rewards 16 ender pearls, which you will need for the Enchanting Table path in `t1_survival`. Build the network early.
