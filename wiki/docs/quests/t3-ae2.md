# Going Digital

**Tier 3 — Chapter 2 of 5**

Applied Energistics 2 collapses your entire item inventory into a searchable digital network. This chapter covers the full setup path: growing Certus Quartz, crafting processors, building an ME Controller, and finally enabling autocrafting — the single biggest quality-of-life jump in the pack.

Requires: `t3_power` quest 4 (Energy Storage) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Certus Quartz** | Collect 16 Certus Quartz crystals from meteorites or underground budding blocks. |
| 2 | **Fluix Fusion** | Throw Charged Certus, Redstone and Quartz into water to fuse them into 16 Fluix Crystals. |
| 3 | **The Inscriber** | Build an Inscriber to stamp Processors using Presses from AE2 meteorites. |
| 4 | **Processors** | Stamp 4 Engineering Processors (gold → Logic, Certus → Calculation, Diamond → Engineering). |
| 5 | **ME Controller** | Place an ME Controller powered by your FE network to serve as your network's brain. |
| 6 | **Your First ME Network** | Connect a Terminal, ME Drive, and two 4k storage cells — your entire inventory in one searchable screen. |
| 7 | **Autocrafting** | Set up Pattern Providers with Molecular Assemblers and encoded patterns for on-demand automated crafting. |

## Tips

- **Explorer's Compass** (from `t1_exploration` quest 2) can locate AE2 meteorites. You need the Inscriber Presses inside them — find all four before starting quest 3.
- **Automate the Inscriber chain** (quest 4) with Create belts or XNet connectors. You will need processors for every AE2 machine and most Tier 4 upgrades.
- Use the terminal search filter `@create` (or `@mekanism`, etc.) to view all items from a single mod at once.
- **Growth Accelerators** (side quest 9) make Certus farming practical. Crystal demand never fully stops, so a small farm with accelerators is always worth the block space.
- Autocrafting (quest 7) transforms the late game. Encode all your common recipes — brass, steel, alloys, essences — so you can request any intermediate item on demand.
