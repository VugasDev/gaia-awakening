# Mass Storage

**Tier 4 — Chapter 2 of 5**

At Tier 4, item volumes grow large enough that standard AE2 storage cells become unwieldy. This chapter introduces MEGA Cells — storage from 1M to 256M items — and the Quantum Network Bridge for seamless cross-dimensional access.

Requires: `t3_ae2` quest 7 (Autocrafting) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Drive Array** | Add 2 more ME Drives with 64k storage cells to scale your base network capacity. |
| 2 | **Enter MEGA** | Craft your first 1M MEGA Cell (built from Sky Steel, found in meteorites) for dramatically larger item storage. |
| 3 | **64M — Practically Infinite** | Craft a 64M MEGA Cell — equivalent to over 1 000 double chests in a single drive slot. |

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |

| **Quantum Bridge** (n=4) | 2 Quantum Entangled Singularities + 10 XP levels | Build a Quantum Network Bridge (8 Quantum Rings) to link your ME network across any distance or dimension. |

## Tips

- **Partition cells** by item type before filling them with bulk materials. Cobblestone and essence on the same unpartitioned cell wastes type slots quickly.
- **Sky Steel** for MEGA Cells is found in AE2 meteorites — the same locations that held your Inscriber Presses. Use the Explorer's Compass from Tier 1 to locate them.
- The **Quantum Bridge** (side quest 4) is essential if your Nether or End operations are far from your ME network. Link them once and manage everything from one terminal.
- The 64M cell (quest 3) has a 16M reward cell component, which helps fund the 256M endgame cell available in `t5_endgame`.
