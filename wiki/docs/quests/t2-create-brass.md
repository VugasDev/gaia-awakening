# The Brass Age

**Tier 2 — Chapter 1 of 4**

Brass unlocks the smart side of Create: programmable funnels, smart sorting, Mechanical Arms, trains, and the Precision Mechanism that gates almost every advanced machine in the pack. This chapter starts the moment you capture a Blaze and ends with a fully automated logistics network.

Requires: `t1_create` quest 11 (Andesite Casing) and `t1_survival` quest 14 (Blaze It).

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Blaze Burner** | Capture a Blaze in an empty Blaze Burner and place it under a Basin to unlock heated mixing. |
| 2 | **Brass!** | Mix copper and zinc in a heated Basin to produce 16 brass ingots — the Tier 2 material. |
| 3 | **Brass Casing** | Craft 8 Brass Casings to unlock precision machines and smart logistics. |
| 4 | **Electron Tubes** | Sand rose quartz then craft 8 Electron Tubes for advanced machine circuits. |
| 5 | **Mechanical Crafter** | Build a 3x3 grid of 9 Mechanical Crafters for automated crafting (needed for the Resource Catalyst later). |
| 6 | **Precision Mechanism** | Craft 4 Precision Mechanisms using a Deployer on a Depot — the keystone component of Tier 2. |
| 7 | **Mechanical Arm** | Build a Mechanical Arm to move items between any connected inputs and outputs automatically. |

## Optional Side Quests (Diamond)

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Smart Sorting** (n=8) | 8 Filters, 150 XP | Craft 4 Brass Funnels and 4 Brass Tunnels to build a sorted item distribution system. |
| **All Aboard!** (n=9) | 32 Track + Create Schedule, 5 XP levels | Lay 16 track sections and build a Train Station to connect locations with Steam 'n' Rails trains. |
| **The Deployer** (n=10) | 8 Electron Tubes, 150 XP | Craft 2 Deployers — essential for Precision Mechanism automation and any repeatable block-placement task. |

## Tips

- The **Blaze Burner** right-click must target a live Blaze in a Nether fortress. The captured blaze needs fuel to stay active.
- **Automate brass production immediately.** You will consume it faster than expected: Electron Tubes, Brass Casings, funnels, tunnels, and the Mechanical Arm all demand large quantities.
- **Precision Mechanism** (quest 6): Ponder the Deployer to see the exact multi-step assembly dance. Automate the chain using belts, depots and a Deployer before trying to hand-craft dozens.
- The **Mechanical Crafter** (quest 5) grid is required for our custom Resource Catalyst recipe in Tier 4. Build it now, automate it, and it will pay off later.
