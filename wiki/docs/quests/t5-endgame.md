# Gaia Awakens

**Tier 5 — Chapter 2 of 2**

With Mythic Catalysts in hand, the final chapter unlocks Tier 4 seeds for industrial material farming, automates catalyst production with Mythic Liquid XP, builds a Mekanism Fusion Reactor for essentially unlimited power, and culminates in crafting the Gaia Awakening Token — the final quest in the book.

Requires: `t5_bosses` quest 7 (Mythic Catalyst) for quest 1; `t5_bosses` quest 6 (Singularity Shards) for quest 2.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Tier 4 Seeds** | Craft an Osmium Seed (or tin, lead, uranium, fluorite) using Mythic Catalysts — your farm now supplies your entire industry. |
| 2 | **Mythic Liquid XP** | Mix Hyper Experience with a Singularity Shard in a heated Create Mixer to brew Mythic Liquid XP for renewable catalyst production. |
| 3 | **Digital Miner** | Build a Mekanism Digital Miner to extract any specific block from a huge radius by filter — retire your pickaxe. |
| 4 | **Fusion Reactor** | Build a Mekanism Fusion Reactor (laser ignition, D-T fuel) for more power than you will ever consume. |
| 6 | **The Gaia Core** | Craft the Gaia Core — a single item uniting tech, magic, farming and conquest disciplines. |
| 7 | **GAIA AWAKENS** *(Gear Milestone)* | Craft the Gaia Awakening Token to complete every quest in the book. |

## Tips

- **Mythic Liquid XP automation** (quest 2): a Create Spout fills the Catalyst Altar with Mythic Liquid XP. Combine this with a Drygmy farm producing Singularity Shards and catalysts become fully renewable without boss fights.
- The **Digital Miner** (quest 3): set a silk-touch filter for ore blocks to collect them for your processing chain. It operates in a huge radius — park it above a vein-rich chunk and let it run.
- The **Fusion Reactor** (quest 4) needs laser ignition, deuterium and tritium fuel. The reactor then produces vastly more power than you started it with. This is the power source that makes the Gaia Core craft practical.
- The **Gaia Core** (quest 6) recipe requires items from every progression branch — Create, Mekanism, AE2, Ars Nouveau, Mystical Agriculture, and Catalyst systems. If any branch is incomplete, this is where you will notice.
- After completing the final quest, the world does not end. Your infrastructure is now essentially unlimited: an excuse to build the fusion-powered flying fortress the quest book mentions.
