# Magical Automation

**Tier 4 — Chapter 4 of 5**

With an Apprentice Spell Book in hand, Ars Nouveau's familiars become available — and they fundamentally change your production options. Drygmys generate mob drops passively, Whirlisprigs tend farms, and Wixies brew potions. The Archmage Spell Book caps the magic tier.

Requires: `t3_magic` quest 6 (Apprentice Graduation) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **The Drygmy** | Craft a Drygmy Charm to summon Drygmys — familiars that channel nearby mobs to produce their drops without killing them. |
| 2 | **Drygmy Farm** | Build a working Drygmy enclosure with captured mobs and a steady Source supply for passive loot generation. |
| 3 | **Archmage** | Upgrade to the Archmage Spell Book for maximum mana capacity and the maximum number of glyph slots. |

---

## Tips

- **Drygmys and bosses**: once you begin Tier 5 and capture a Cataclysm Apex boss with a Drygmy, the farm generates Mythic Catalyst materials passively. Set the farm up in Tier 4 so it is ready when Tier 5 starts.
- Source generation for the Drygmy farm must be continuous. An Agronomic Sourcelink from a large crop field or an Enchanting Apparatus setup is recommended.
- The **Archmage Spell Book** (quest 3) opens the full Ars Nouveau glyph library. Design your combat and utility spells now before entering Tier 5 boss encounters.
- **Whirlisprigs** (side quest 4) placed over Rich Soil Mystical Agriculture fields effectively remove the need for manual harvesting of your entire farm.
