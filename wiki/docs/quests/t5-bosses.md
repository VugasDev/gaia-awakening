# Apex Predators

**Tier 5 — Chapter 1 of 2**

The Cataclysm mod adds four Apex bosses that are the hardest encounters in Gaia Awakening. Each requires specific preparation and grants Singularity Shards — the raw material for Mythic Catalysts. A milestone gate quest confirms you are ready before the chapter opens.

Requires: `t4_mekanism` quest 3 (Advanced Alloys), `t4_ae2_mega` quest 3 (64M Cell), and `t4_ars` quest 2 (Drygmy Farm) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Ready for Tier 5** *(Gear Milestone)* | Acknowledge that you have socketed gear, high-tier enchantments, buff foods, and a respawn plan. |
| 2 | **Netherite Monstrosity** | Defeat the Netherite Monstrosity in Cataclysm's Soul Black Smith dungeon — ranged attacks and fire resistance essential. |
| 3 | **Ignis, Lord of Fire** | Defeat Ignis in the Burning Arena, a phase-shifting duelist with shields and flame combos. |
| 4 | **The Harbinger** | Defeat the Harbinger in a Ruined Citadel — a hovering machine that fires lasers and missiles. |
| 5 | **Ender Guardian** | Defeat the Ender Guardian deep in the Ruined Fortress of the End — gravity attacks and teleport punishes. |
| 6 | **Singularity Shards** | Collect 4 Singularity Shards dropped by Apex bosses (or farmed passively via a Drygmy enclosure). |
| 7 | **Mythic Catalyst** | Craft a Mythic Catalyst at the Catalyst Altar using Mythic Liquid XP and Singularity Shards. |

## Tips

- **Read quest 1 carefully**: Recommended gear is socketed with Mythic gems, enchanted to pressure tier, with a full set of high-tier Mekanism or Ars Nouveau armor.
- **Boss prep order**: Netherite Monstrosity → Ignis → Harbinger → Ender Guardian. Each is harder than the last.
- **Netherite Monstrosity** (quest 2): stay mobile, use fire resistance potions, and attack from range when possible. Its lava phase punishes melee fighters who stand still.
- **Ignis** (quest 3): it has a shield phase. Learn the attack pattern before committing to DPS windows. Flame resistance lets you ignore the floor hazards.
- **Drygmy farm shortcut** (quest 6): capture an Apex boss in your Drygmy enclosure and it will generate Singularity Shards passively. Set this up in Tier 4 and the shards will already be waiting when you arrive here.
