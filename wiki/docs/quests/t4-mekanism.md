# Heavy Industry

**Tier 4 — Chapter 1 of 5**

With your AE2 network and XNet infrastructure in place, Mekanism's advanced processing tier is now viable. This chapter upgrades ore output from x2 to x3, crafts the powerful advanced alloys, produces Refined Obsidian (gating our custom Drill Heads), and offers some of the most capable personal equipment in the pack.

Requires: `t3_ae2` quest 7 (Autocrafting) and `t3_power` quest 5 (XNet) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Purification Chamber** | Build a Purification Chamber and supply it with oxygen from an Electrolytic Separator for x3 ore clumps. |
| 2 | **The x3 Ore Chain** | Automate the full chain: Purification Chamber → Crusher → Enrichment Chamber → smelter for 3 ingots per ore. |
| 3 | **Advanced Alloys** | Craft 8 Reinforced Alloys (diamond infusion) and 4 Atomic Alloys (Refined Obsidian) for endgame machines. |
| 4 | **Refined Obsidian** | Produce 8 Refined Obsidian Ingots — required for Atomic Alloys and the custom Refined Obsidian Drill Head. |

## Tips

- The **Electrolytic Separator** splits water into hydrogen and oxygen. Hydrogen powers the Jetpack; oxygen feeds the Purification Chamber. One separator can serve both.
- The x3 chain (quest 2): Raw Ore → Purification Chamber (clumps) → Crusher (dirty dust) → Enrichment Chamber (dust) → furnace (ingot). Automate this with XNet or AE2 channels.
- **Atomic Alloys** (quest 3) require Refined Obsidian, which requires Refined Obsidian Ingots (quest 4). These gate the Digital Miner and Fusion Reactor in Tier 5 — prioritize them.
- The **Atomic Disassembler** (side quest 6) with vein mode and FTB Ultimine together constitute what the quest calls "geological war crimes." Highly recommended before Tier 5 boss preparation.
