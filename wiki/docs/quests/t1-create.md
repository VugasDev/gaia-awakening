# The Andesite Age

**Tier 1 — Chapter 2 of 3**

This chapter is your introduction to the Create mod, which forms the backbone of progression in Gaia Awakening. Starting from raw andesite and iron, you will build rotating machines, automate ore processing, and unlock every major Create concept — shafts, wheels, presses, mixers, crushing wheels, belts, and funnels — before graduating to the Brass Age.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Andesite Alloy** | Craft 8 Andesite Alloy (andesite + iron or zinc nuggets) — the cornerstone of all Create machines. |
| 2 | **Shafts and Cogs** | Craft 8 shafts and 8 cogwheels to learn how Create transmits rotation. |
| 3 | **Water Power** | Build 3 Water Wheels in flowing water to generate your first stress capacity. |
| 5 | **Press On** | Build a Mechanical Press and a Depot to start producing iron sheets for recipes. |
| 6 | **Mixing It Up** | Add a Mechanical Mixer and Basin to automate combination recipes. |
| 7 | **Crushing It** | Build 2 Crushing Wheels to pulverize ores for bonus output. |
| 9 | **Belts and Funnels** | Connect machines with 4 Belt Connectors and 4 Andesite Funnels to automate item flow. |
| 11 | **Andesite Casing** | Craft 8 Andesite Casings to complete the andesite engineering phase and unlock Tier 2. |

## Tips

- **Ponder any Create block** (hover over it and press W) for an interactive 3D tutorial. This is the fastest way to learn placement rules.
- The Water Wheel (quest 3) requires flowing water on multiple faces. More flow faces = more stress capacity. Arrange water sources carefully.
- The Mechanical Press (quest 5) needs a Basin below it. Iron sheets appear in dozens of Create recipes — automate this output immediately.
- **Crushing Wheels** (quest 7) need to spin toward each other. Build one pair early and route crushed ore through an Encased Fan over water for the "Double Your Ores" bonus before you move on.
- Andesite Casings (quest 11) formally close Tier 1 Create. You will need blaze rods from `t1_survival` quest 14 to start the Brass Age.
