# Chef's Table

**Tier 2 — Chapter 4 of 4**

Farmer's Delight turns food from a survival afterthought into a genuine buff system. Proper meals grant Nourishment — passive saturation regeneration — which matters enormously in long boss fights and deep mining sessions.

Requires: `t1_survival` quest 5 (Farm to Table) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Sharp Knife, Sharp Mind** | Craft an Iron Knife and a Cutting Board to double ingredient yield from crops. |
| 2 | **Rich Soil** | Produce 16 Rich Soil by composting organic material — crops grow faster on it and it never needs re-tilling. |

---

## Optional Side Quests (Diamond)

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Feast Mode** (n=3) | 16 Golden Carrots + 2 Cakes, 150 XP | Cook any multi-ingredient meal in the Cooking Pot to earn the Nourishment buff before a tough fight. |

---

## Tips

- **Rich Soil** (quest 2) is the most important reward from this chapter. Mystical Agriculture seeds in Tier 3 grow significantly faster on Rich Soil than on regular farmland.
- The **Knife + Cutting Board** combo (quest 1) often yields two portions from one ingredient, effectively doubling your food supply without extra farming effort.
- The **Nourishment buff** from Feast Mode stacks with golden carrots, meaning you can sustain indefinitely in combat without ever eating mid-fight.
- Keep a stockpile of Rich Soil ready before you start Mystical Agriculture — you will want to cover entire fields with it.
