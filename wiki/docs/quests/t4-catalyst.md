# The Catalyst

**Tier 4 — Chapter 3 of 5**

The Resource Catalyst is a custom item created for Gaia Awakening that unlocks Tier 3+ Mystical Agriculture seeds. Three acquisition routes exist — passive drops, crafting, and an automated Altar — and a companion Drill Head system upgrades your Create Ore Excavation mining. This is the pack's deepest custom system.

Requires: `t3_ma` quest 4 (Tier 2 Seeds) and `t2_create_brass` quest 5 (Mechanical Crafter) to begin quest 1; `t2_create_brass` quest 6 (Precision Mechanism) to begin quest 3.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Resource Catalyst** | Obtain your first Resource Catalyst via a passive Ore Excavation drop, a Mechanical Crafter recipe with essences, or the Catalyst Altar. |
| 2 | **Tier 3 Seeds** | Use the Resource Catalyst to craft a Tier 3 seed (diamond, emerald, gold, or glowstone) — your farm now grows wealth. |
| 3 | **Vein Mining** | Build a Create Ore Excavation Drill and Vein Atlas to tap infinite ore veins for resources (and catalyst drop chances). |
| 4 | **Osmium Drill Head** | Craft the custom Osmium Drill Head to upgrade your excavation drill speed — the first of five head tiers. |
| 5 | **Obsidian Upgrade** | Craft the Refined Obsidian Drill Head for faster veins and better catalyst drop rates in Tier 4. |

## Tips

- The **three catalyst routes** are designed for different playstyles: mining casually (passive drops), crafting with automation (Mechanical Crafter), or investing in the Altar infrastructure (most scalable). Do not ignore the Altar if you plan to farm Tier 4 seeds at volume.
- The **Vein Atlas** (quest 3) is required to locate ore veins for the Drill. Keep it in your hotbar while exploring.
- **Drill Heads** are interchangeable on the Excavation Drill. Swap in the Crystal Head for AE2 meteorite excavation or the Nether Head for ancient debris runs.
- The Altar of Power (side quest 8) connects directly to the Tier 5 endgame: Mythic Liquid XP (from `t5_endgame` quest 2) uses the same Altar infrastructure with Singularity Shards.
