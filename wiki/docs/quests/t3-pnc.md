# Under Pressure

**Tier 3 — Chapter 3 of 5**

PneumaticCraft runs on compressed air rather than Forge Energy, requiring its own infrastructure. That infrastructure pays off: the Pressure Chamber is the foundation of our custom over-limit enchanting system, and PneumaticCraft machines open routes to Tier 4 progression.

Requires: `t2_mekanism` quest 4 (Enrichment Chamber) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Compressed Iron** | Make 8 Compressed Iron Ingots by dropping iron near TNT and detonating it — applied explosive metallurgy. |
| 2 | **Air Compressor** | Build an Air Compressor with 8 Pressure Tubes to supply compressed air to machines. |
| 3 | **Pressure Chamber** | Construct a hollow cube of 16 Pressure Chamber Walls with a Valve and 2 Interfaces for pressure crafting. |

---

## Optional Side Quests (Diamond)

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Vortex of Cool** (n=4) | 8 Blue Ice, 125 XP | Craft a Vortex Tube to split compressed air into hot and cold sides for temperature-sensitive machines. |
| **Sky Shopping** (n=5) | 12 Emeralds, 150 XP | Craft an Amadron Tablet to order materials via drone delivery — check for advantageous trade offers regularly. |

---

## Tips

- **Security Upgrades** for the Air Compressor are not mandatory, but an overloaded pressure network will explode. Install them before cranking up output.
- The Pressure Chamber (quest 3) makes Compressed Iron without explosives — safer and repeatable.
- The Pressure Chamber is the entry point to the **custom pressure enchanting system** in Tier 4 (`t4_enchant` quest 4). Building and understanding it now prepares you for overpowered enchantments later.
- **Amadron Tablet** (side quest 5): some drone offers trade for items you are naturally generating in excess. Window-shop the tablet regularly — occasionally the deals are exceptional.
