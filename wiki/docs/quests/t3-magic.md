# School of Magic

**Tier 3 — Chapter 5 of 5**

Ars Nouveau is the primary magic system in Gaia Awakening. You design custom spells from modular glyphs, build Source-powered machines, and eventually summon familiars that automate farming and item logistics. This chapter takes you from the introductory Worn Notebook to an Apprentice Spell Book ready for Tier 4.

Requires: `t1_survival` quest 8 (Enchanted) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **The Worn Notebook** | Craft the Worn Notebook (book + lapis) to unlock your in-game magical reference. |
| 2 | **Novice Spellbook** | Craft a Novice Spell Book and a Scribes Table to begin designing custom spells from glyphs. |
| 3 | **Source of Power** | Build an Agronomic Sourcelink near growing crops and place 2 Source Jars to store magical energy. |
| 4 | **Imbuement Chamber** | Craft an Imbuement Chamber to convert lapis and amethyst into Source Gems. |
| 5 | **Enchanting Apparatus** | Build an Enchanting Apparatus with Arcane Pedestals to craft advanced Ars gear and charms. |
| 6 | **Apprentice Graduation** | Upgrade to an Apprentice Spell Book for stronger spells and more mana capacity. |

## Tips

- **Glyph design** is freeform: combine Form + Effect + Modifier glyphs at the Scribes Table. Start with Projectile + Harm (a basic magic bolt) and build from there.
- The **Agronomic Sourcelink** needs growing crops nearby — plant a small wheat or sugarcane patch next to it for continuous Source generation.
- **Starbuncles** (side quest 7) are a magical item transport alternative to XNet. They are slower and personality-driven but require no energy infrastructure.
- **Iron's Spellbooks** (side quest 8) scrolls are most commonly found in When Dungeons Arise structures and Mowzie boss loot. The Explorer's Compass can help locate those structures.
- The **Archmage Spell Book** (Tier 4, chapter `t4_ars`) is the final spellbook tier. Plan your glyph progression so you are not rebuilding your spell library from scratch at each upgrade.
