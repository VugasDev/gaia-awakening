# First Steps

**Tier 1 — Chapter 1 of 3**

This chapter walks you through the fundamentals every Gaia Awakening run demands: gathering wood and iron, surviving the first nights, reaching diamonds, building an Enchanting Table, and finally crossing into the Nether. It is the longest early-game chapter, covering the full arc from the crafting table to blaze rods.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Welcome to Gaia** | Craft a Crafting Table and collect oak logs to get your bearings in this taller, wilder world. |
| 2 | **Fire and Ore** | Build a Furnace and smelt 8 iron ingots — the first step of every Minecraft civilization. |
| 3 | **Iron Will** | Craft a full set of iron armor (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots) to survive the night. |
| 7 | **Diamonds!** | Dig below Y=16 to collect 3 diamonds; the world extends to Y=−128, so the riches go deep. |
| 8 | **Enchanted** | Place an Enchanting Table with 15 bookshelves — Apotheosis overhauls this system significantly. |
| 12 | **Set a Waypoint Home** | Craft and place a Waystone to enable fast travel across the enormous world. |
| 13 | **Into Hell** | Gather 10 obsidian and a Flint and Steel, then open a Nether portal. |
| 14 | **Blaze It** | Hunt blazes for 4 blaze rods and collect 16 Nether quartz — required for the Brass Age. |

## Tips

- The world uses **Tectonic + Terralith**: mountains reach Y=512 and caves are massive. Mark your base with a Waystone (quest 12) before exploring far.
- **Apotheosis** reshapes the enchanting system. More bookshelves unlock better options, and the pressure-enchanting system (Tier 4) pushes levels far beyond vanilla maximums.
- If you skip "Farm to Table," you will miss **Rich Soil** — this matters later when Mystical Agriculture seeds need faster growth.
- Blaze rods (quest 14) gate the entire Tier 2 Create chapter. Do not skip the Nether.
