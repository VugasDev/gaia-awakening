# Green Machines

**Tier 2 — Chapter 2 of 4**

Mekanism brings energy-driven machinery with a clean power interface, dedicated ore processing, and an excellent quality-of-life toolset. This chapter takes you from osmium mining through steel production to x2 ore processing and your first energy storage.

Requires: `t1_survival` quest 7 (Diamonds) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Osmium — The New Iron** | Mine and collect 16 osmium ingots — Mekanism's foundation metal found underground everywhere. |
| 2 | **Metallurgic Infuser** | Build the Metallurgic Infuser, which infuses metals with carbon, redstone, and diamond to create alloys. |
| 3 | **Steel Yourself** | Produce 16 steel ingots (iron + enriched carbon twice) to build Steel Casings for all Mekanism machines. |
| 4 | **Enrichment Chamber** | Build an Enrichment Chamber to begin x2 ore processing and enrichment for infusion. |
| 5 | **The x2 Ore Chain** | Produce 32 iron dust by running raw iron through the Enrichment Chamber — twice the metal per ore. |
| 6 | **Stored Energy** | Craft a Basic Energy Cube to store and distribute Forge Energy across your machines. |

---

## Optional Side Quests (Diamond)

| Quest | Rewards | What to do |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Heat Generator** (n=7) | 1 Lava Bucket, 125 XP | Build a Heat Generator and surround it with lava blocks for free passive energy trickle. |
| **Tanks for Everything** (n=8) | 4 Buckets, 100 XP | Craft a Basic Fluid Tank for portable, upgradeable liquid storage (sneak + right-click for bucket mode). |
| **The Configurator** (n=9) | 8 Osmium Ingots, 100 XP | Craft a Configurator to set machine input/output sides and build automatic pipe-free chains. |

## Tips

- **Osmium** can also be obtained from Create's crushing wheels — our custom Crushed Osmium recipe smelts into ingots, so integrate this with your crusher early.
- The **Enrichment Chamber** (quest 4) requires a Precision Mechanism from `t2_create_brass`. Plan both chapters in parallel.
- Use the **Configurator** (side quest 9) to set machines to auto-eject into the next stage. With careful side configuration you can chain Metallurgic Infuser → Enrichment Chamber → smelter without any external pipes.
- The Basic Energy Cube (quest 6) connects to Create Crafts and Additions generators in Tier 3. Start thinking about your power layout now.
- **Steel Casings** are the chassis of every Mekanism machine. Craft two or three times what you think you need.
