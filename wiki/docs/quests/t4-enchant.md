# Beyond Vanilla Enchanting

**Tier 4 — Chapter 5 of 5**

This chapter dismantles the vanilla enchanting ceiling. Apotheosis gems add stat bonuses from mob drops. Create: Enchantment Industry liquifies XP for industrial-scale enchanting. And our custom pressure enchanting system uses PneumaticCraft pressure chambers to push enchantment levels far beyond what any vanilla table can reach.

Requires: `t3_pnc` quest 3 (Pressure Chamber) to begin.

---

## Main-Path Quests

| # | Quest | Aim |
|---|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Gem Hunter** | Collect 3 Apotheosis Gems dropped by monsters — each has stat bonuses scaled by rarity. |
| 2 | **Socket to Me** | Use a Sigil of Socketing to add gem sockets to your gear and slot in your best gems. |
| 3 | **Liquid Knowledge** | Build a Create Enchantment Industry Experience Rotor to harvest XP from item belts and convert it to liquid. |
| 4 | **Pressure Enchanting** | Use the custom PneumaticCraft Pressure Chamber system to push enchantments past vanilla caps. |

---

## Optional Side Quests

None — all four quests are on the main path.

---

## Tips

- **Apotheosis gem rarity** follows a tier from Common to Mythic. Higher rarity gems can double damage output or provide near-immunity to specific damage types. Always compare before socketing.
- **Hyper Experience** (from quest 3) is required for the Catalyst Altar (`t4_catalyst` side quest 8). Building the Experience Rotor serves both systems simultaneously.
- The **pressure enchanting tiers** correspond to bar levels in your PneumaticCraft system: 3.5 bar, 5.5 bar, and 7.5 bar unlock progressively higher enchantment levels up to what the game calls GODLIKE.
- Use the **Enchantment Library** (from Apotheosis) to store extracted enchantments from unwanted gear and combine them later. This is how you accumulate max-tier enchantments efficiently.
- Socketed gear with Mythic gems and pressure-pushed enchantments is the recommended preparation before entering Tier 5 boss encounters.
