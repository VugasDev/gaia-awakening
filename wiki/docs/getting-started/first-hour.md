# Your First Hour

Here is what to focus on when you first load into Gaia Awakening.

## Open the Quest Book

Press **[** (default) to open the FTB Quests book. This is your primary guide — every major step in the
pack has a quest explaining what to do and why. Work through Tier 1 before trying to figure out the rest.

See [Using the Quest Book](quest-book.md) for a full explanation of chapters, quest shapes, and rewards.

## The Tier 1 Path

Tier 1 is split into three chapters that you progress through in parallel. None of them require the others
to finish first, but the quest dependencies within each chapter tell you the recommended order.

### Chapter 1: First Steps

The survival foundation. The key quests in order:

| Quest | What you do |
|-------|------------|
| Welcome to Gaia | Craft a crafting table, collect wood — the usual start |
| Fire and Ore | Build a furnace and smelt your first 8 iron ingots |
| Iron Will | Craft a full iron armor set before night makes it painful |
| Diamonds! | Mine at least 3 diamonds (below Y=16; the world goes to Y=-128) |
| Enchanted | Build an Enchanting Table with 15 bookshelves — Apotheosis overhauls this system |
| Set a Waypoint Home | Place a Waystone at your base for fast travel |
| Into Hell | Build a Nether portal — you need blaze rods for the next tier |
| Blaze It | Kill blazes for 4 blaze rods and collect 16 quartz |

Diamond-shaped quests in this chapter (sleeping, cooking with Farmer's Delight, FTB Ultimine vein mining,
Sophisticated Backpacks) are optional side content with good rewards — do them when convenient.

### Chapter 2: The Andesite Age

Your first steps with Create. These run in parallel with Chapter 1 and are the direct prerequisite for
Tier 2.

| Quest | What you do |
|-------|------------|
| Andesite Alloy | Craft 8 Andesite Alloy (andesite + iron or zinc nuggets) |
| Shafts and Cogs | Craft shafts and cogwheels — the basic Create vocabulary |
| Water Power | Place 3 Water Wheels in flowing water for your first power source |
| Press On | Build a Mechanical Press with a Depot below it |
| Mixing It Up | Build a Mechanical Mixer with a Basin |
| Crushing It | Build two Crushing Wheels for ore doubling |
| Belts and Funnels | Connect machines with belts and funnels |
| Andesite Casing | Craft 8 Andesite Casings — this completes the Andesite Age |

!!! tip
    Hover over any Create block and press **W** (Ponder) for an interactive 3D tutorial built into the game.

### Chapter 3: Into the Wild

Exploration runs alongside the other two chapters and rewards you with maps, compasses, artifacts, and
boss kills.

| Quest | What you do |
|-------|------------|
| Nature's Compass | Craft Nature's Compass to locate any of the 100+ Terralith biomes |
| Explorer's Compass | Craft Explorer's Compass to find structures and dungeons |
| Ferrous Wroughtnaut | Defeat Mowzie's underground boss — your first real boss fight |

The Frostmaw, Dungeon Delver, and Artifact Hunter quests in this chapter are all optional side content.

## Key Mods to Know in T1

- **Create** — everything mechanical: power, processing, transport. Use Ponder (W) liberally.
- **Apotheosis** — the enchanting overhaul. Its additional bookshelf types unlock higher enchantment levels.
- **Farmer's Delight** — optional but rewarding; Nourishment buff from cooked meals is noticeably useful.
- **JourneyMap** — press **J** for the full world map. Press **M** for the minimap config.
- **FTB Chunks** — press **M** (FTB map, separate from JourneyMap) to claim chunks and protect your base.
- **Waystones** — activate any Waystone you find in a village or dungeon; they are shared across the group.

## What to Avoid Early

- **Mystical Agriculture seeds** — you cannot plant T3 or T4 seeds without the Catalyst items, which require
  significant progression. Don't chase those early.
- **Spending diamonds on armor** — wait until you have Apotheosis socket gems before committing good diamonds
  to armor pieces.
- **Skipping the Nether** — you need blaze rods before you can enter the Brass Age (Tier 2). Plan the trip
  before you run out of iron.
